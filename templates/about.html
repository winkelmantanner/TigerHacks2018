This website is a news browsing website that was made for TigerHacks 2018. We get our sources of news from the NewsApi. Then we have a bot that calculate how much each article agrees with the others. The user is able to search for any type news as they want in our search function. The "agreement scores" are computed by comparing the articles to each other. The higher the agreement score, the more the article matches the information in the other articles. The agreement score can be negative.
